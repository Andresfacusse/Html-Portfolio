The Best Movies According to Andy

My top 3 favorite movies of all-time.

Redline (2009)

A story about the most popular and dangerous race in the Galaxy. Hand drawn animated masterpiece!

Moulin Rouge (2001)

My girlfriend's favorite romantic film and musical.

Whiplash (2014)

A story about an aspirant drummer eager to take his place amongst the best in the world and a teacher doing his best to find the next big music star. A film about pressure and how not to give up.
